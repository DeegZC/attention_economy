Scenes of Attention



Welcome to the final project of "The Attention Economy: Historical Perspectives" for the Spring 2021 semester!


What is this?

For this project, we collected "Scenes of Attention", which are images in which some form of attention is "active."

We sought to identify different approaches to understanding, defining, and realizing attention across the human experience and its histories.


How do I use it?

We offer two modes for navigating these images: random and chronological. Random displays a random image and chronological allows you to browse through the images chronologically.

Single-click on the image to flip it and read its caption. Double-click to enlarge the image and inspect it more closely.


Special thanks to Dr. David Graham Burnett for guiding us through this semester and helping us develop the analytical tools for the search and captioning process for these images!
